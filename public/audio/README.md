# Ambient audio drop-in (ElevenLabs)

Put your ElevenLabs-generated soundscape or brand voiceover here as:

    public/audio/ambience.mp3

When present, a small gold sound toggle appears at the bottom-right of the hero
(`components/AudioToggle.jsx`). Audio never autoplays with sound (browsers block
that) — the visitor taps to start it, which is the correct luxury-site behaviour.

Ideas for the track (generate in ElevenLabs):
- A soft, airy ambient pad with a single distant chime (Sound Effects / Music).
- A 6–10s whispered brand line: "Maison Lumière — the invisible art of presence."
- Loopable, ~30–60s, MP3, quiet mix (the toggle plays it at 50% volume).
